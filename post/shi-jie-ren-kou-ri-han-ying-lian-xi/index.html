世界人口日汉英练习

2020-02-06

《2030年可持续发展议程》是为所有人在一个健康的星球上创造更美好未来制定的世界蓝图。值此世界人口日，我们认识到，这一使命与人口增长、老龄化、移民和城市化等人口趋势密切相关。
The 2030 Agenda for Sustainable Development is the world's blueprint for a better future for all on a healthy planet. On World Population Day, we recognize that this mission is closely interrelated with demographic trends including population growth, ageing, migration and urbanization.
划重点：
1.2030年可持续发展议程 The 2030 Agenda for Sustainable Development
2.世界人口日 World Population Day
3.相关 be interrelated with
4.老龄化 aging

翻译感想：
当句子很长时，找到核心，理清逻辑在往上加修饰语。

虽然世界人口总体上继续增长，但这种增长是不平衡的。对于世界上许多最不发达国家而言，可持续发展面临的挑战因人口快速增长以及易受气候变化影响而变得更加复杂。其他国家正面临人口老龄化的挑战，需要促进健康的积极老龄化、提供充分的社会保护。
While the world's population overall continues to increase, this growth is uneven. For many of the world's least developed countries, the challenges to sustainable development are compounded by rapid population growth as well as vulnerability to climate change. Other countries are facing the chanllenge of ageing populations, including the need to promote healthy active ageing and to provide adequate social protection.
划重点：
5.因……加剧 be compounded by
6.vulnerability-vulnerable-open to attack or damage, 表达负面影响的新说法

随着世界城市化进程不断深入，预计到2050年将有68%的世界人口生活在城市，可持续发展和气候变化将越来越依赖于对城市增长的成功管理。在管理这些人口趋势的同时，我们还必须认识到人口、发展和个人福祉之间的关系。25年前，在开罗国际人口与发展会议（人发会议）上，世界各国领导人首次阐明了人口、发展和人权之间的关系。他们还认识到，促进性别平等既是正确的做法，也是实现可持续发展和改善所有人福祉的最可靠途径之一。
As the world continues to urbanize, with 68% of the world population projected to live in urban areas by 2050, sustainable development and climate change will increasingly depend on the successful management of urban growth. While managing these population trends, we must also recognize the relationship between population, development and individual well-being. Twenty-five years ago, at the Cairo International Conference on Population and Development (ICPD), world leaders first spelt out the links between population, development and human rights. They also recognized that promoting gender equality is both the right thing to do and one of the most reliable pathways to sustainable development and improved well-being for all.
划重点：
7.预计 be projected to
project最常见的意思是项目，英文是a specific plan or design,
所以有预计的意思也好理解了，to plan, figure, or estimate for the future
8.阐明 spell out=clarify, make plain
9.途径 pathway

今年的世界人口日呼吁全球关注开罗人发会议尚未完成的事务。11月，开罗会议二十五周年纪念峰会将在内罗毕举行。我鼓励会员国参加最高级别的会议，并作出坚定的政治和财政承诺，以实现人发会议的《行动纲领》。推进实现人发会议的愿景将为那些掉队的人提供机会，并有助于为所有人的可持续、公平和包容性的发展铺平道路。
This year's World Population Day calls for global attention to the unfinished business of the Cairo ICPD Conference. In November, a summit marking the 25th anniversary of the Cairo Conference will take place in Nairobi. I encourage Member States to participate at the highest levels and to make firm political and financial commitments to realize the Programme of Action of the ICPD. Carrying forward the vision of the ICPD will unlock opportunities for those left behind and help pave the way for sustainable, equitable and inclusive development for all.
划重点：
10. ……是……周年纪念日, 动词可以用mark, fall on
11. 举行 这里翻的时候用的主动时态 take place
12. 地理常识：开罗是埃及首都，内罗毕是肯尼亚首都 Cairo-Eygpt Nairobi-Kenya
13. 行动纲领 一听到纲领就想到guideline，感觉在说纲领的时候可以通用。programme是program的英式写法，用作纲领英文解释是a plan or system under which action may be taken toward a goal
14. 提供机会这里翻成了unlock opportunities,有释放机会的意思，会员国对那些欠发达国家减少限制，让他们有机会。
15. 所有人的 for all 放到句子末尾，以介词形式表述定语
